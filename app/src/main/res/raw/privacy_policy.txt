MindHarmony Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2025

1. Introduction
Welcome to MindHarmony. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe, supportive environment for teenagers seeking mental health support. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1. Personal Information
When you use our app, we collect:

- Username (displayed in the app)

- Notification preferences

2.2. Mental Health Data
To provide personalized support, we collect:

- Mood tracking entries

- Journal entries

- Wellness plan preferences

- Interactions with the AI chatbot

- Meditation and focus mode usage

2.3. Usage Information
We automatically collect certain information about your device and how you use MindHarmony, including:

- App usage statistics

- Device type and operating system

- App version

- Crash reports

3. How We Use Your Information
We use your information to:

- Provide and improve our services

- Personalize your experience

- Generate insights about your emotional patterns

- Send notifications and reminders you've enabled

- Ensure the safety and security of our users

- Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security
Your mental health data is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your personal information. Note that if you uninstall the app, all your data will be permanently deleted.

5. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregated data in the following circumstances:

- Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our app

- Safety Concerns: If we believe disclosure is necessary to prevent harm (see Crisis Detection Policy)

- Legal Requirements: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or legal processes

6. Crisis Detection and Response
MindHarmony includes features designed to detect signs of severe emotional distress or suicidal ideation. If our system detects such content:

- We'll provide immediate resources for support

- We will not automatically contact emergency services or others without your explicit consent

- In extreme cases and where required by law, we may need to share information with appropriate authorities

7. Your Rights
You have the right to:

- Access the data stored on your device

- Clear all your data through the app settings

- Uninstall the app, which will delete all associated data

8. Parents and Guardians
If you are under the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction, you must have permission from a parent or guardian to use MindHarmony. Parents/guardians should supervise their child's use of the application.

9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@mindharmony.app
